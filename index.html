MAK SYNTH

A hands-on synthesizer kit for learning sound design, electronics, and music production.

About

Mak Synth is a hardware synthesizer designed to make sound design physical and intuitive. Built using Arduino, custom electronics, 3D printed components, and SuperCollider, the project aims to teach synthesis through direct interaction rather than software menus.
